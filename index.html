About me

Hi everyone! My name is Razzan Favian FWH. I'm a web developer from Bandung, West Java. I have 1 year of experience in back-end web development. I really enjoy what I do right now, in my opinion, creating programs is not just a job, but also an art that has aesthetic value.

My job is to build your website to be functional and user-friendly yet still attractive. In addition, I provide a personal touch to your product and ensure that the website catches attention and is easy to use. My goal is to convey your message and identity in the most creative way.
